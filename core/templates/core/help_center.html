Buying on Matrix

How do I search for products?

Use the search bar at the top of the page or browse by categories. You can also use filters to narrow down your results.

How do I contact a supplier?

Click the "Contact Supplier" button on any product page. You can call directly or send a WhatsApp message.

Is my purchase protected?

Yes! All purchases are covered by our Order Protection program, including money-back guarantee and shipping protection.

Selling on Matrix

How do I list an item?

Click "Sell Item" in the navigation, fill out the form with your item details, upload photos, and publish your listing.

What are the selling fees?

Basic listings are free. We only charge a small commission when you successfully sell an item.

How do I get verified?

Complete your profile, provide business documentation, and maintain good seller ratings to become verified.
